# Annual Data Collection Guide

Ask these questions **one at a time** and save each answer to memory before moving on.
Use `memory_user_edits` tool to save after each answer.

---

## Question List

### Q1 — EIN & ITIN
"What is your LLC's EIN and your personal ITIN?"
- Save as: `[LLC Name] EIN: XX-XXXXXXX. Owner: [Name], ITIN: XXX-XX-XXXX`

### Q2 — P&L / Financial Data
"Do you have your [YEAR] Profit & Loss ready? Please share revenue and each expense line."
- Key items to capture: revenue, foreign operations, bank charges, commissions,
  software/subscriptions, rent, contract labor, travel, any other expenses
- Total assets = year-end cash balance in LLC bank account
- Save full P&L breakdown to memory

### Q3 — Information Returns
"Did you receive or issue any 1099-NEC, 1099-K, W-2, or 1042-S forms for [YEAR]?"
- Usually "none" for remote foreign-owned LLCs
- Save: `[LLC] [YEAR]: No 1099s/W-2s/1042-S received or issued` OR list them

### Q4 — Payroll
"Did the LLC have any U.S. employees or payroll in [YEAR]?"
- Usually "No" for remote foreign-owned LLCs
- Save: `[LLC] [YEAR]: No U.S. employees, no payroll, no 941s or W-2s`

### Q5 — Form 5472 Related-Party Transactions
"Tell me about any money that moved between you personally and the LLC in [YEAR]:"
- Prompt with examples:
  a) Did you take any money out for personal use? (distributions)
  b) Did you put your own money in? (contributions/loans)
  c) Did the LLC pay any of your personal expenses? (travel, rent, etc.)
  d) What is the current balance in the company account?
- Save each transaction type and USD amount

### Q6 — Addresses
"Which address should we use on each form?"
- U.S. registered office → Form 1120 & 5472
- Foreign personal/office address → Form 1040-NR
- Confirm both addresses are current

### Q7 — 1040-NR Filing Facts
Ask three sub-questions:
- a) Filing status (usually Married Filing Separately for married NRAs)
- b) Days physically present in the U.S. during [YEAR]
- c) Any U.S.-[country] tax treaty benefits claimed? (usually No)

### Q8 — Schedule OI Facts
- Citizenship country
- Country of residence for tax purposes
- U.S. visa type used during visits (B1/B2, F-1, etc.)
- Did they file a U.S. tax return for prior year? What form?

### Q9 — Prior Filing History
"Were any [YEAR] returns previously filed or are these completely new?"
- Note any corrections needed
- Save: `Prior [YEAR] returns: [filed/abandoned/fresh start]`

### Q10 — State Returns
"Did the LLC conduct business in any specific U.S. state, or entirely remote from abroad?"
- Usually no state returns for remote foreign-owned LLCs
- Save: `State returns: [required/not required] — [reason]`

### Q11 — Payment
"Have you made any estimated tax payments? How will you pay any tax due?"
- Check IRS online account balance at irs.gov/account
- Save: `[YEAR] estimated payments: [amount or none]. Payment via: [method]`

---

## Memory Template

After collecting all data, ensure memory contains:
```
1. [LLC] EIN: XX-XXXXXXX. Owner: [Name], ITIN: XXX-XX-XXXX
2. [LLC] [YEAR] P&L: Revenue $X. Expenses: [list]. Net income: $X
3. Information returns: [none / list]
4. Payroll: [none / details]
5. Form 5472 transactions: [list with amounts]
6. Addresses: [US address] for 1120/5472. [Foreign address] for 1040-NR
7. 1040-NR: Filing status [MFS]. Days in U.S.: [X]. Treaty: [Yes/No]
8. Schedule OI: Citizenship: [country]. Visa: [type]. Prior return: [form]
9. Prior returns: [status]
10. State returns: [status]
11. Payment: [estimated payments]. Method: [IRS online / check]
```
